Driven by Discovery: The ToriCalc Story

Unveiling the passion, purpose, and principles behind ToriCalc.

Making Complex Chemical Engineering Accessible

Empirical study in chemical engineering shouldn't be an insurmountable barrier. ToriCalc is born from the conviction that complex calculations can be made straightforward, transparent, and empowering for everyone.

Our unique approach: ToriCalc doesn't just provide answers; we offer step-by-step solutions, often in clear LaTeX format. This empowers you to truly learn, understand the underlying methodologies, and confidently apply these critical methods in your academic and professional endeavors.

Chemical engineering stands on the shoulders of giants and countless empirical studies. We are dedicated to meticulously translating solvers from these vital research papers and foundational textbooks, making them widely accessible and ensuring every contribution is recognized and leveraged for future learning and innovation.

A Dedication Born from Universitas Indonesia

ToriCalc's foundation lies in a profound appreciation for empirical study, cultivated through the rigorous and inspiring learning process at Universitas Indonesia. The challenging yet rewarding academic environment there was the crucible where this vision was forged.

This journey ignited a passion not just for mastering these intricate chemical engineering principles, but for creating a universally accessible tool. ToriCalc embodies the ambition to democratize knowledge, empowering students, researchers, and professionals in chemical engineering across the globe with the confidence to tackle complex problems.

Guided by Principle

Free for All

We firmly believe that essential engineering tools and knowledge should be available to everyone, everywhere, without financial barriers. ToriCalc is, and always will be, completely free to use.

Openness & Transparency

Transparency is fundamental to learning and trust. We champion openness—from our commitment to open-source code (where applicable) to providing clear, step-by-step methodologies for every calculation.

Community Driven

ToriCalc is built for the chemical engineering community, by a member of it. Our aim is to serve, support, and evolve based on the feedback and needs of students, educators, and professionals in the field.

Constantly Evolving, Always Improving

Our journey with ToriCalc is one of continuous growth and relentless improvement. We are passionately committed to expanding our library of solvers, drawing from the rich and ever-evolving landscape of chemical engineering research. New methodologies, refined models, and broader applications are always on our horizon.

The vision extends further: to develop a comprehensive suite of 'ChemE Tools'—practical, powerful software and resources designed to directly support and enhance the work and studies of chemical engineering students and professionals worldwide. From data analysis aids to process simulation utilities, we aim to equip the next generation of engineers.
